About NavMenta

Empowering careers through AI-driven innovation

Our Mission

At NavMenta, we believe everyone deserves a fulfilling career path. Our mission is to democratize career development by leveraging artificial intelligence to provide personalized guidance, mentorship, and opportunities for professionals at every stage of their journey.

Our Story

Founded in 2024 by SK Group of Companies, NavMenta emerged from a shared frustration with the limitations of traditional career services. We saw how talented individuals were struggling to navigate the rapidly changing job market and decided to build a solution that combines human expertise with cutting-edge technology.
